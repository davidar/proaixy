DOAI

DOAI (Digital Open Access Identifier) is an alternate DOI (Digital Object Identifier) resolver that takes you to a free version of the requested article, when available.

To use it, replace dx.doi.org by doai.io in any DOI link.

Examples

  • http://doai.io/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2015.09.023 vs
    http://dx.doi.org/10.1016/j.jalgebra.2015.09.023
  • http://doai.io/10.1139/f92-220 vs
    http://dx.doi.org/10.1139/f92-220

Who runs this

DOAI is run by CAPSH, and relies on the metadata provided by our partners, most notably the Bielefeld Academic Search Engine (BASE).
